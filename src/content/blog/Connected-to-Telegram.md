Hisenz.com has now been connected to telegram. 
This feature is powered by IFTTT's RSS feed service, forwarding new posts to telegram chats or channels. However, if you prefer RSS reading, the feed is available at [https://hisenz.com/rss2.xml](https://hisenz.com/rss2.xml).
