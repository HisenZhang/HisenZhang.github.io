Https, HTTP over SSL, is one of the encrypted protocols which is widely used in web serving. The reason people would like to trust https connection is that HTTP protocol transfers non-encrypted contents. Activities such as online shopping cannot be secured under such a condition, so https was born.

Hisenz.com now has https connection working to deliver every word from this site to your browser.
