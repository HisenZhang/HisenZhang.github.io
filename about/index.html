关于我 / Bio

我是海森，现就读于伦斯勒理工学院的计算机科学与计算机工程双专业本科，专注于数字通信与人工智能技术。业余无线电呼号KD2TAI，研究兴趣包括软件定义无线电、数字信号处理，以及无线/物联网安全。

Hisen, KD2TAI, officially known as Zhemin Zhang, is the president of W2SZ Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute Amateur Radio Club. He is pursuing a B.S. in Computer Science dual Computer and Systems Engineering, focusing on digital communication, artificial intelligence, and wireless/IoT security.
