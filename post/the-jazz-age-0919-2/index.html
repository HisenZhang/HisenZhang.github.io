背景 / Background

这本Verve原始音乐专辑（横跨1947年至1953年）收到大量推荐，这不仅是因为它拥有现代爵士乐创作者的精湛音乐才能，而且还考虑了录音的音质。派克的许多发行作品都是由于设计不善的现场录音或听众偷窃而选出的，因此需要忍受如此多的声音嘶嘶声和外来的噪音，以至于无法与欣赏相提并论。这些Verve音轨并非如此，它们的音阶清晰且平衡良好，彰显了Parker令人眼花technique乱的技巧以及完整，敏锐的音色。

This compilation of original Verve sessions (spanning 1947-1953) comes highly recommended, not only for its earful of superb musicianship from the creators of modern jazz, but for the sound quality of the recordings. Many Parker releases, culled from poorly engineered live recordings or audience bootlegs, require tolerating so much sonic hiss and extraneous noise that it distances and distracts from the enjoyment. Not so with these Verve tracks, which are astonishingly clear and well-balanced throughout, highlighting Parker’s dazzling technique and full, incisive tone to good measure.

由Miles Davis，Dizzy Gillespie，Lester Young，Thelonious Monk和Max Roach等人加入，Bird和合奏通过“ Mohawk”，“ Now’s the Time”和“ Laird Baird”（派克写给他的儿子）。应该警告那些对管弦乐过敏的人，包括“ Laura”，“ April in Paris”和“ Just Friends”（其中包括据说是帕克的个人最爱）等几首歌可能听起来难以忍受。帕克的演奏超越了一切，而这本专辑对于粉丝和新来者来说都是一个杰出的收藏。

Joined by the likes of Miles Davis, Dizzy Gillespie, Lester Young, Thelonious Monk, and Max Roach, among others, Bird and ensemble blaze through versions of “Mohawk,” “Now’s the Time,” and “Laird Baird” (which Parker dedicated to his son). Those allergic to orchestral arrangements should be forewarned that several tracks, including “Laura,” “April in Paris,” and “Just Friends” (which includes the solo Parker reputedly claimed was his own favorite), are string-laden. Parker’s playing transcends all, however, and Bird: The Original Recordings is an outstanding collection for fans and newcomers alike.

Monk是爵士乐界的巨人之一，是路易斯·阿姆斯特朗，戴夫·布鲁贝克和埃林顿公爵之后以及温顿·马萨里斯之前在《时代》周刊上发表的五位爵士音乐家之一。这张专辑展示了Monk的独特即兴创作风格，表现出​​他的不谐调和棱角分明的旋律曲调，这与Monk对钢琴的非正统方法相吻合，同时具有高度的打击乐性以及对沉默和犹豫的戏剧性使用。这是任何爵士黑胶唱片收藏的绝佳补充。

One of the giants in jazz, Monk is one of five jazz musicians to have been featured on the cover of Time, after Louis Armstrong, Dave Brubeck, and Duke Ellington, and before Wynton Marsalis. This album showcases Monk’s trademark unique improvisational style, featuring his dissonances and angular melodic twists that were consistent with Monk’s unorthodox approach to the piano along with his highly percussive attack and abrupt dramatic use of silences and hesitations. A great addition to any jazz vinyl collection.

巴黎音乐会：第二版是爵士钢琴家比尔·埃文斯（Bill Evans）与马克·约翰逊（Marc Johnson）和乔·拉巴贝拉（Joe LaBarbera）的现场唱片，于1979年在法国巴黎录制，最初以Elektra / Musician唱片发行。 这场音乐会的其他唱片作为《巴黎音乐会：第一版》发行。

斯科特·亚诺（Scott Yanow）的《 Allmusic》评论授予专辑4.5颗星，并指出：“音乐敏锐而微妙，令人兴奋……这证明他在过去的一年中表现出了新的生命力和热情，而不是下降。

The Paris Concert: Edition Two is a live album by jazz pianist Bill Evans with Marc Johnson and Joe LaBarbera recorded in Paris, France in 1979 and originally released on the Elektra/Musician label. Additional recordings from this concert were released as The Paris Concert: Edition One.

The Allmusic review by Scott Yanow awarded the album 4½ stars and states “The music is sensitive and subtly exciting… and serves as evidence that, rather than declining, he was showing a renewed vitality and enthusiasm in his last year”.
