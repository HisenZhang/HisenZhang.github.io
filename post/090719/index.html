爵士时代 | VOL. 1 - Chet Baker in Tokyo

Posted on 2019-09-07 | In Music |

Chet Baker是我最喜欢的一位爵士乐手. 他的小号演奏技巧和声音让人不由自主的迷失. 听Chet的作品, 应当陪衬着昏暗的灯光与陈年威士忌.

曲目列表 / Tracklist

  1. Broken Wing
  2. I’m a Fool to Want You
  3. Stella by Starlight
  4. For Minors Only
  5. You’d Be so Nice to Come Home to
  6. Arborway
  7. Four
  8. Almost Blue
  9. Beatrice
  10. My Funny Valentine
  11. Seven Steps to Heaven

未播放的曲目 / Not Played in This Show

Two more songs not played in this show:

  1. For All We Know
  2. Portrait in Black and White

背景 / Background

这张唱片在他去世仅11个月前释出，被誉为“切特·贝克（Chet Baker）暮色中的光辉时刻”。

Released only eleven months before his death, it has been hailed as, “A glorious moment in Chet Baker’s twilight.”
